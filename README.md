# Bottleneck 🐛💻

Welcome to **Bottleneck**! This project is our very first GameJam experience, built by a passionate 4-person development team. 

When presented with the jam's theme, **"Harmony"**, we decided to interpret it from a technical perspective: The delicate balance and synergy between computer hardware components. 

### 🎮 The Concept
A computer can only perform as well as its weakest link. In *Bug Butcher*, your goal is to manage a PC system by maintaining absolute harmony between the **CPU**, **GPU**, and **RAM**. 

If you upgrade one component too much while neglecting the others, you will disrupt the system's harmony and cause a **Bottleneck** (Darboğaz). A bottleneck drastically reduces your system's efficiency multiplier, making it harder to reach your ultimate goal of 100% system completion.

### ✨ Features & Gameplay Mechanics
* **Hardware Mini-Games:** Dive into component-specific interfaces to play classic retro mini-games (like Snake, Pong, and Fishing) to gather progress and "bugs" (currency).
* **The Harmony Bar:** Keep a close eye on your system's harmony percentage. Keep your CPU, GPU, and RAM levels balanced to keep the harmony high!
* **Bottleneck Penalty:** If the level gap between your components gets too high, your multiplier drops. A Level 2 system with 100% harmony works much faster than an unbalanced system!
* **Upgrades:** Spend your collected resources to level up your components, unlocking new mini-games and higher multipliers.

### 🕹️ Controls
* **Movement:** `W`, `A`, `S`, `D` keys
* **Interact/Proceed:** `Left Mouse Click`

### 🛠️ Built With
* **Game Engine:** [Godot Engine 4](https://godotengine.org/)
* **Language:** GDScript
* **Art & Audio:** Custom assets and sound effects crafted for the jam.

### 🚀 How to Run
1. Download the latest release from the Releases tab or clone the repository.
2. If running from source, open the project in Godot Engine 4.x.
3. Run the `main_menu.tscn` to start the game. Keep the harmony, squash the bugs, and don't let your PC bottleneck!
